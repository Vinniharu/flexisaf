About Me

I am a passionate and skilled frontend developer with a strong foundation in Computer Science, currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree at Caleb University. My journey in web development has been marked by a series of impactful internships and projects that have honed my expertise in React, JavaScript, and modern web technologies.

With hands-on experience at companies like Upperlink Limited, BuildHubb, Keep Fresh, and MacRonics, I've contributed to developing responsive web applications, optimizing performance, and enhancing user experiences. My work has resulted in tangible improvements, such as a 20% boost in SEO ratings and a 60% increase in customer base for an e-commerce platform.

I specialize in creating efficient, reusable React components and have a keen eye for UI/UX design. My technical toolkit includes React.js, Next.js, TypeScript, and state management with Redux. I'm also proficient in styling with Tailwind CSS and integrating frontend solutions with backend APIs and Firebase.

My portfolio showcases a range of projects, from global information web apps to educational platforms, demonstrating my ability to translate complex requirements into user-friendly interfaces. I'm particularly proud of my senior project, a study material resource web platform that leverages React and Firebase to support university students.

As a dedicated learner and innovator in the field of frontend development, I am eager to bring my technical skills, creative problem-solving abilities, and passion for fintech to a challenging internship role in the banking sector. I am committed to driving innovation and contributing to the evolution of digital banking experiences.
